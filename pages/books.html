When I was younger and was studying at CNSD, my father always gave me money for the book fair - though he never gave me money to buy lunch at school (had to bring something from home) or for video games. Then when I got older, he would take me to bookshops and tell me that I could choose anything. I remember when I hesitantly got Peter Norvig's Artificial Intelligence from one of the shelves. It was over 300 BRL (lot of money for a book in Brazil). He bought it without thinking, and told me that what I was learning with the books would stay with me forever.

That's how I ended up with piles of books that I had to give away or sell when I moved to New Zealand. This page contains a list that started with a database used to sell the books online.
